Three Thing Game Jam

A game created within 24 hours based on the words Playing, Going and Glue. The game jam took place at my University during April 2019.

About Three Thing Game

Three Thing Game is a game jam that runs twice a year at my University. You are given 3 random words from a random word generator, and you have to make a game based on those 3 words over the weekend. You have the option of working as a solo team or a team up to the size of 5. The 3 words I got were Playing, Going and Glue and I decided to work solo as I wanted to use the Roblox Studio engine.

My game "Glue Attack"

For my game, I decided to use my surrounding area and mix the idea of a glue monster and three thing game. The basic idea of my game is that you are a student taking part in Three Thing Game (Also known as 3TG). However, you find out that the lab where the event is taking place (Fenner Lab) has been taken over by a Glue Monster. Your goal is to free all the other students captured by the monsters and then defeat big boss, who has captured David (The person who is running the event).

The player is given a red laser gun, which is used to free the students from the glue. However, the Glue Monsters can also fight back by shooting glue at the player. This causes the player to receive damage and their walk speed is reduced. During testing of the game, I found that the game was quite hard, even with the glue damage reduced. To fix this, I added in a medic box, where whenever a glue monster is killed, it would drop the box and heal the player.

The game is split into 3 rounds. Round 1 is designed to be a fairly easy level, where the player gets more used to the controls. Round 2 is where the walk speed and damage of the glue monster is increased, making the game harder. On round 3, the boss appears with more glue monsters. When you have killed all the glue monsters, the boss forcefield is removed and you can start attacking the boss until half health. Then the boss with spawn in more glue monsters. and you will have to kill all the monsters again. After this, you can now fully kill the boss and David will appear to tell the player that they have saved Three Thing Game.
